Reaching IIT (BHU)

From the Babatpur Airport or from the Cantt./ Manduadeeh railway station, there are a number of ways to get to the city and to Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Banaras Hindu University campus. The most convenient way is to take Taxi/Cab. 

There are taxi calling stations at the front of all arrival levels at all airport terminals or railway station. Pre-paid Taxis are also available. Ola Radio Taxis are also available. 

Upon boarding a taxi, simply tell the driver your hotel with address or IIT (BHU) as destination, and the driver should take you to your destination accordingly. Travellings times are about 60 to 90 minutes. IIT (BHU) campus is about 40 Kms. from Varanasi (Babatpur) Airport and AC Taxies usually charge about Rs.1000/- INR for a sedan car.
