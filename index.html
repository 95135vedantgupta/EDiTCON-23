International Conference on Distributed Computing and Networking

(ICDCN 2019)


ICDCN is a premier international conference dedicated to addressing advances in Distributed Computing and Communication Networks. Over the years, it has become a leading forum for disseminating the latest research results in these fields. The 20th edition of this conference will be organized in the Department of Computer Science and Automation, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India. Incidentally, the Department of Computer Science and Automation of IISc also celebrates its golden jubilee in the year 2019. In addition, Bangalore is not only the home of many industries in India in both public and private sector, but also a major tourist attraction. One can also enjoy the other tourist attraction points around Bangalore such as Mysuru, Waynad, Ooty etc.



ICDCN 2019 will be organized in two tracks: Distributed Computing and Networking, and will comprise of a highly selective technical program consisting of refereed regular and short papers, panel discussions as well as focused workshops on emerging topics. See Call for Papers for more details. Conference proceedings will be published in the ACM Digital Library.


Selected papers will be invited for fast-track publication in one or more appropriate high-quality journals. The conference will consider awarding a Best Paper Award, independently for each track.



Important Note: For an accepted paper to appear in the ICDCN proceedings, at least one author of the paper in the main conference/workshops must register as a regular registrant even if he/she happens to be a student, and the paper must be presented in the ICDCN 2019 conference by one of its authors.
